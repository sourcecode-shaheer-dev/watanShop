App Privacy

Welcome to Watan Shop (“we,” “our,” or “us”). Your privacy matters — this page explains what we collect, why, and how you can control your information when using the Watan Shop mobile app and related services.

1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you access or use Watan Shop (the “App”). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App you accept the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register or make a purchase, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
Non-Personal Information

We may also collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage data and interaction statistics
  • Location data (if you enable location services)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Process and fulfill orders, and manage transactions.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Send order updates, promotions, or app notifications (you may opt out).
  • Improve the app’s performance and user experience.
  • Detect and prevent fraud and protect the security of users.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data in a limited set of situations:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors (payment processors, delivery partners, analytics providers) who help operate our services.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, or to respond to lawful requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, sale, or transfer of assets, personal data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational measures to help protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no system can be completely secure — please also take measures to protect your account (e.g., choose a strong password).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the details below. We will respond within applicable legal timeframes.

7. Third-Party Services

Our App may use third-party services (e.g., analytics SDKs, payment processors). These services have their own privacy policies — we recommend reviewing them to understand how they collect and use your data. Examples include payment gateways and delivery partners.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember user preferences, enable features, and collect analytics to improve the App. You can control cookies via your device settings. Our cookie banner allows you to accept or decline non-essential tracking.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods will vary depending on the type of data and legal requirements.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes we will notify users via the App or other prominent notice. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.
